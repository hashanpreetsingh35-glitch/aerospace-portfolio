LRT Rail Stop Redesign

Redesigned a Light Rail Transit station stop to improve safety, passenger flow, and structural efficiency.

Methods

Conducted structural analysis of existing LRT station infrastructure, evaluated passenger flow patterns and accessibility requirements. Developed ergonomic design concepts for improved safety and efficiency, including platform layout optimization and structural reinforcement analysis.

Tools Used

SolidWorks for 3D modeling and design visualization, CATIA for advanced CAD modeling, ANSYS for structural finite element analysis and simulation.

Outcome

Created comprehensive redesign proposals with enhanced passenger safety features, improved accessibility, and optimized structural efficiency. Delivered detailed design recommendations and analysis reports for potential implementation in LRT systems.
