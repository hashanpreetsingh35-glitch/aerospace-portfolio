V-2500 Turbofan Reverse Engineering

Reverse engineered the V-2500 turbofan to validate aerodynamic performance and turbine stage design.

Methods

Utilized mean-line analysis for preliminary turbomachinery design, focusing on compressor and turbine stage performance. Conducted CFD simulations to validate aerodynamic behavior, including blade profile optimization and stress analysis. Performed detailed component matching to ensure overall engine balance and efficiency.

Tools Used

ANSYS Fluent for CFD analysis, MATLAB for design calculations, CATIA for 3D modeling and blade design, MDIDS-GT for turbomachinery performance prediction.

Outcome

Designed optimized turbine stages with improved aerodynamic efficiency and validated compressor performance against reference data. Generated comprehensive design documentation including blade profiles, stress analysis, and performance maps for the V-2500 turbofan.
